Review

Ava Morgan

The Blood Request Form is well-designed and user-friendly. The use of a red color scheme effectively aligns with the theme of blood donation, creating a strong visual impact. The form covers all the essential fields such as patient details, blood group, hospital information, and contact number, ensuring that the necessary data is collected. The layout is clean, with clear labels and well-spaced input fields, making it easy to navigate. The inclusion of a consent checkbox is a thoughtful touch for user acknowledgment. Overall, the form is functional, visually appealing, and serves its purpose effectively.

Sophia Bennett

While the Blood Request Form is functional and visually appealing, there are areas for improvement. Adding an email field would provide an alternative method of communication. Introducing an urgency selection (Normal, Urgent, Critical) could help prioritize requests. Additionally, integrating form validation to check for incomplete or incorrect inputs would enhance user experience. Implementing a success message after submission would also give users confirmation that their request has been received. Lastly, adding a CAPTCHA could help prevent spam submissions.

About

Blood donation is a voluntary procedure where a person donates blood to help save lives. Donated blood is used for surgeries, accident victims, cancer patients, and those with blood disorders. A single blood donation can save up to three lives.
